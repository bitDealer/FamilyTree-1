Use an ArrayList<Person> to store everyone.
Within Person:
	Attributes:
	<String>
		- Name
		- Date of Birth
		- Birthplace
	<Integer>/<String>
		- Date Married
		- Date Divorced
	<Person>
		- Partner
		- Mother
		- Father
	<ArrayList<Person>>
		- Children
	<HashMap<Person, Integer[2]>>
		- Divorcees
	<Boolean>
		- Adopted
		- Alive
